WIHOA

The WIHOA (Wasatch Ice Hockey Officials Association) services the ice hockey officiating needs for Utah. WIHOA officials have worked some of the highest levels of hockey and have been known to enjoy each others company away from the rink. Come and join us, we would love to have you!
